I am an undergraduate student in the School of Chemical Engineering at
[Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU)](https://www.skku.edu), Suwon, Korea. My work
centers on applying machine learning and data analytics to chemical and
manufacturing processes, with a particular interest in smart-factory systems,
process control, and renewable-energy technologies.

Research Interests
======
- Engineering process control
- Data preprocessing and analysis
- Renewable energy technology
- Machine learning for smart factory
